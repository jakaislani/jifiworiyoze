    DeVito began his acting career on the stage, which helped launch his Hollywood career when he recreated his off-Broadway role of Martini in the film adaptation of One Flew Over the Cuckoo’s Nest (1975), co-starring Jack Nicholson.

    Despite its success, the film did not propel DeVito to stardom as he had intended. That wouldn’t happen for another few years when he took over as Louie DePalma on Taxi in 1978.
