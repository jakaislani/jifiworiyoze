    Keir Simmons (born 22 February 1972) is an English journalist. Since August 2012, he has been a correspondent for NBC News.He was previously UK Editor for ITN' s ITV News, where he covered stories including the 2004 Indian Ocean earthquake and tsunami, the 7 July 2005 London bombings and the 2006 death of Russian Alexander Litvinenko. In 2003, he was nominated for a Royal Television Society Award. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
