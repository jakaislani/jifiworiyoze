    Career

    After completing her degree in acting, NYYXXII decided to follow her passion for gaming and started streaming on Twitch on 18 June 2020. She was affiliated with the platform on 2 July 2020, and the monetization for her content began on 29 January 2021 when she became a Twitch partner. She has more than 200,000 followers on her Twitch account and most of her streams of gaming. She began streaming with her favorite game, Little Nightmares.
