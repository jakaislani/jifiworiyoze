    Who made the stand arrows?

    The Stand Arrows don’t actually have to be arrows to affect people. The power of the Arrow originally came from a meteorite which happened to land on Earth 50,000 years ago. It’s this meteor that the Arrows were eventually fashioned from, by a man who wanted to achieve godhood through the powers of the meteorite.

    How does the stand arrow work?

    The Arrow’s power awakening a Stand When a living creature is pierced by one of the Arrows, it will catalyze and awaken the individual’s dormant Stand. The reason the Arrows can awaken Stand abilities is because the meteorite the Arrows were made from was host to an alien virus.
