    UME 2016 Announces Porter Robinson, Galantis, And More

    For its sixth iteration, the Ultimate Music Experience is returning to the island of South Padre, TX with its most impressive year to date. Phase one of UMEs spring break partyhas just been announced, and the three-day festival is positively stacked.

    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    For its sixth iteration, the Ultimate Music Experience is returning to the island of South Padre, TX with its most impressive year to date. Phase one of UME’s spring break party has just been announced, and the three-day festival is positively stacked.

    Recommended Videos

    2016 will see Porter Robinson, Dmitri Vegas & Like Mike, Galantis, Jauz, and Sam Feldt join the lineup, alongside Marshmello, Borgeous, and 3LAU. Additionally, the event’s pre-party on March 16th will host Jackal, Big Gigantic, DJ Snake and Keys & Krates. Yeah, it’s going to be a big year.

    And of course, being on an island, rest assured there’ll be plenty of water sports and activities.

    Ultimate Music Experience is scheduled to take place March 16-19. Tickets are available now, so be sure to grab them if you haven’t done so already.
