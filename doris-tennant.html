    Doris Tennant is the celebrity kid, widely famous as the third born kid, a daughter of entertainment personalities Georgia Tennant (mother) and David Tennant (father).

    The celebrity daughter Doris was born in September 2015. As of now, she is living cheerfully with her parents and siblings. Unlike other celebrity kids, Doris is kept far away from the paparazzi. She also rarely features on her parent's social media posts.

    Parent's Professional Work

    Doris was born silver spoon in her mouth. Since day one of birth, she is receiving constant media attention. All credit goes to her beloved and celebrated mother, Georgia, and her father, David.

    The 36-years-old Georgia Elizabeth Moffett is an English actress better known for her appearance in the TV series. Moffett has starred in series like Doctor Who, Merlin, Doctor Who Greatest Moments, Loose Women, and others. She has also served as a producer for the films You, Me and Him, 96 Ways To Say I Love You, and series The Five (ish) Doctor Robots.

    On the other hand, Doris' dad David is a Scottish actor. Tennant is better known for his role as Barty Crouch Jr in the 2005 fantasy film Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. Jacqueline Luesby's daughter Emma Watson has played a leading role in the movie. 

    The Emmy Awad-winning David's other movie and TV shows credit includes The Pirates! In an Adventure with Scientists! Mad To Be Normal, Mary Queen of Scots, Casanova, Dead Ringers, United, Jessica Jones, DuckTales, Einstein and Eddington. 

    In A Marital Relationship Since 2011

    The actor David and an actress Georgia first met one another in 2008 while filming Doctor Who's episode, 'The Doctor's Daughter.' In the show, Georgia has portrayed a role as Jenny, and David has worked as a doctor. 

    After the first encounter, Moffett and Tennant shared a good bond and also start their romantic affair. Later, it was revealed that the duo got engaged in January 2011.

    Following several months of engagement, Georgia and David decided to take their relationship to the next level and got married on December 30, 2011. The duo tied the wedding knot privately. So, detailed information regarding the marriage lacks.

    As of 2021, Mr. and Mrs. Tennant are celebrating their 10yh marriage anniversary blissfully. The husband-wife duo attends many high-profile events together. There is a 13-years of age gap between the married pair. However, they are ignoring it and are sharing a good bond without involving in any signs of separation.

    Shares Good Bond With Siblings

    Doris has three siblings, sisters Olive Tennant (b. 2011), Birdie Tennant (2019), and a brother named Wilfred Tennant (2013). She shared a good bond with her siblings.

    Like Doris, her sisters and brother are also gaining media recognition as celebrity children. They are also kept far away from the spotlight. Besides Olive, Wilfred, and Birdie, Doris also has a half-sibling. 

    Doris's half-brother Ty Peter Tennant was born on March 2, 2002, via Georgia's former temporary relationship with a mysterious man. Later, David adopted Ty. As of now, he is living happily with his mother, Georgia, father David, and four siblings.

    The 18-years-old Ty Peter is also following in his parent's footsteps. He is a struggling actor who has already worked in the film Tolkien and the series War of the Worlds. Additionally, Ty is also famous on Instagram, he has amassed more than 25 thousand followers on his official Instagram account (@ty_tenzo).
